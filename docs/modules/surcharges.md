# Module: Surcharges

The surcharges module manages configurable surcharges applied to orders.

---

## Responsibilities

- Configurable surcharge rules.
- Surcharge types: card surcharge, Sunday surcharge, public holiday surcharge, service charge, delivery fee, packaging fee, bottle deposit, environmental levy.
- Taxability of surcharges.
- Order-level and line-level surcharge application.
- Surcharge display on receipt.

## Key Rules

- Surcharges are configurable, not hard-coded.
- Surcharge taxability must be configured per surcharge type.
- Surcharges are audited.
