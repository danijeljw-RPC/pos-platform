# Module: Reporting Service

The reporting service provides sales, tax, payment, and operational reports.

See also: `docs/modules/14-reporting-customers-gift-cards.md`.

---

## Responsibilities (MVP)

- Daily sales report.
- Sales by payment method.
- Sales by category.
- Sales by product.
- Sales by staff member.
- Sales by terminal.
- Sales by location.
- Refunds report.
- Voids report.
- Discounts report.
- Tax report (GST summary).
- Cash reconciliation / end-of-day report.

## Responsibilities (Later)

- Executive dashboards.
- CSV/Excel export.
- Accounting software export (Xero, MYOB).
- Stock movement report.
- Gift card liability report.
- Tips/gratuity report (US/CA).
