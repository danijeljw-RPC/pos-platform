# Module: Inventory Service

The inventory module manages stock levels and movements.

See also: `docs/modules/12-kitchen-routing-inventory.md`.

---

## Responsibilities (MVP)

- Finished item stock counts.
- Sold-out state per product/location.
- Stock decrement on sale.
- Manual stock adjustments.
- Waste/spoilage recording.
- Daily stock reset.
- Low-stock alerts.

## Responsibilities (Later)

- Ingredient/component inventory.
- Bill of materials / recipe costing.
- Serial number tracking (electronics/repair).
- Purchase orders and supplier management.
- Stocktake workflow.
