# Module: Customer Service

The customer module manages customer profiles and purchase history.

See also: `docs/modules/14-reporting-customers-gift-cards.md`.

---

## Responsibilities (MVP)

- Customer profile (name, email, phone).
- Purchase history.
- Store credit.

## Responsibilities (Later)

- Loyalty points/stamps.
- Digital receipts delivery.
- Cross-location customer profile.
- Tax exemption (for business customers).
- Customer groups for pricing.
