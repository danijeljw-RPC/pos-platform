# Module: Gift Cards

The gift card module manages gift card issuance, redemption, and liability tracking.

---

## Responsibilities (Later)

- Gift card issuance.
- Gift card redemption (as a payment method).
- Gift card balance tracking.
- Gift card liability ledger.
- Cross-location gift cards (later).
- Gift card receipt.

## Status

Not in MVP scope. Planned for Phase 2.

## Related Modules

- Payments (gift card as payment method)
- Audit (gift card activity audited)
