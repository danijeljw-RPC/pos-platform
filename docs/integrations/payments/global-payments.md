# Integration: Global Payments

## Overview

Global Payments is an international payment technology company with a broad range of merchant services, integrated POS solutions, and terminal hardware. Global Payments has presence in AU/NZ, APAC, US, UK, and EU.

## Status

Planned — adapter not yet implemented. Target for global expansion.

## Region

AU, NZ, APAC, US, UK, EU, and others.

## Priority

Phase 4 (global expansion).

## Notes

- Global Payments offers unified payment APIs across multiple regions.
- Suitable for large enterprise and franchise groups.
- Global Payments also owns TSYS, a major US payment processor.

## Integration Points

- `DaxaPos.PaymentProviders.GlobalPayments`
- Implements `IPaymentTerminalProvider`.
